And...cut! The design process behind animation tools

This talk explores the unconventional and evolving design processes behind the animation tools at Pixar and Walt Disney Animation Studios. Through case studies, we'll showcase how innovative approaches to collaboration and iterative design address the ever-changing needs of artists, ultimately shaping tools that fuel the creation of groundbreaking films. Gain valuable insights and best practices for adopting flexible design processes that empower expert users—who not only rely on these tools but actively contribute to their development.

The design opportunities of moving to Houdini for lighting within the world of Animation

Lighting plays a key role in our films. For Walt Disney Animation Studios' "Moana 2", we developed a new Lighting workflow in Houdini, empowering artists with more control while reducing creative barriers. This talk explores how we enable new workflows, mirror successful experiences, and ease the transition with new tools in a legacy system.

Behind the Frames: Designing Artist-Tailored Animation Tools

What does it take to build tools as flexible as the stories they help create? Discover how we design proprietary animation tools at both Pixar and Walt Disney Animation Studios. We'll share our collaborative, artist-tailored approach to crafting adaptable workflow tools - a focus on nimble, practical strategies that both fuel our iconic films and offer our expert users the kind of tools they need to grow and evolve with their work.
